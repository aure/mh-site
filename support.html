Support

We're here to help you get the most out of Metronome Hero.

Contact Us

For questions, bug reports, or feature requests, email us at support@metronomehero.com. We typically respond within 24 hours.

Getting Started

  • MIDI input — Connect a MIDI controller or electronic drum pad, then select MIDI as your input mode in Settings > Input.
  • Microphone input — Grant microphone access when prompted. The app detects note onsets in real time to score your timing.
  • Tap input — Tap anywhere on the visualization area to register hits. Great for practice without an instrument.

Subscription & Billing

Metronome Hero offers a 7-day free trial. After the trial, a monthly or annual subscription is required. Subscriptions are managed through your Apple ID.

  • To manage or cancel your subscription, go to Settings > [your name] > Subscriptions on your device.
  • Payment is charged to your Apple ID account. We do not have access to your payment information.

Troubleshooting

  • No sound — Check that your device is not in silent mode and that the volume is turned up. Verify the click sound is enabled in Settings > Metronome.
  • MIDI not detected — Ensure your MIDI device is connected and recognized by your device. Try disconnecting and reconnecting.
  • Microphone not working — Check that microphone permission is granted in your device's Settings > Privacy > Microphone.
  • Subscription not recognized — Tap "Restore Purchases" in Settings > Subscription within the app.

Privacy

Metronome Hero processes all audio and MIDI data on your device. No personal data is collected or transmitted. Read our full Privacy Policy.
